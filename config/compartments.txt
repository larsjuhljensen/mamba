[CONTENT:ABOUT]
<div style="width: 50em;">
<p>
COMPARTMENTS is a frequently updated web resource that integrates evidence on protein subcellular localization from manually curated literature, high-throughput screens, automatic text mining, and sequence-based prediction methods. We further unify the evidence by assigning confidence scores that facilitate comparison of the different types and sources of evidence and visualize these scores on a schematic cell.
</p>
<p>
For further details please refer to following the Open Access article about the database:
</p>
<p>
<a href="http://database.oxfordjournals.org/content/2014/bau012.full">COMPARTMENTS: unification and visualization of protein subcellular localization evidence</a><br />
</p>
</div>
